Business Overview

A well-established Italian restaurant operating from a prime CBD location, with a loyal customer base built over nearly a decade. The business benefits from consistent walk-in traffic as well as a strong repeat patronage from nearby office workers and residents.

The fully equipped kitchen features professional-grade Italian appliances capable of supporting a high-volume service. The 60-seat dining room is designed to accommodate both casual dining and private group bookings, which contribute meaningfully to overall revenue. Strong brand presence in the Central Business District supported by consistent Google and TripAdvisor ratings.

Reason for sale: Owner is relocating overseas for personal reasons and wishes to ensure business continuity under new management. Full handover support and staff retention planned during transition.
