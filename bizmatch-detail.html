Business Overview

This is a profitable, bootstrapped SaaS platform serving the HR and workforce management needs of Singapore's corporate sector. Built over five years, the platform has grown to serve over 200 corporate clients across industries including finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and professional services.

The product covers the full HR lifecycle: payroll, leave management, performance reviews, onboarding workflows, and compliance reporting tailored for Singapore's MOM requirements. The tech stack is modern — React frontend, Node.js API, PostgreSQL — and hosted on AWS with 99.9% uptime SLAs.

The current owner is pursuing other business interests and is committed to a transition period of up to 12 months to ensure continuity. The product has no technical debt backlog that would require immediate capex, and the engineering team of 4 is willing to remain post-acquisition.

Reason for sale: Founder relocation and new personal venture. Not a distress sale — revenue and client base are both stable and growing.
