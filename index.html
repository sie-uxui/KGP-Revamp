FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Key Global Projects offer?
We offer Business Valuation, M&A Planning, IPO Strategies, Corporate Structuring, Business Modernisation, and our AI Advisory Platform. Each service is tailored to help Singapore and regional businesses access capital markets and grow sustainably.
How long does an IPO process typically take?
A U.S. Nasdaq listing typically takes 12–18 months from initial preparation to listing day. However, a Reverse Takeover (RTO) can significantly shorten this timeline by acquiring an existing listed shell company.
What is a Reverse Takeover (RTO)?
An RTO involves acquiring a publicly listed shell company to gain a stock exchange listing without going through the traditional IPO process. It is faster, more cost-effective, and a proven path we have successfully executed for our clients.
Do you work with companies outside Singapore?
Yes. While we are Singapore-incorporated and have deep local expertise, we work with clients across Malaysia, China, Hong Kong, and internationally. Our bilingual capability in English and Mandarin is a key advantage for cross-border transactions.
How is a business valuation conducted?
Our valuation process involves a comprehensive assessment of your financials, assets, market position, and growth potential. We produce a structured valuation report that can be used for investor presentations, M&A negotiations, or share issuance purposes.
What is BizMatch and how does it work?
BizMatch is our business matching marketplace where buyers and sellers of Singapore businesses can connect. Browse available listings by industry and price range, and our team will facilitate introductions and advisory throughout the transaction.
How do I get started with Key Global Projects?
The easiest way is to fill in our free consultation form on this page, or speak directly with our AI Advisor chatbot for an initial assessment. One of our directors will respond within 24 hours to discuss your goals and the best path forward.
How much does your advisory service cost?
Our fees vary depending on the scope and complexity of your project. We offer a free initial consultation to understand your needs before providing a transparent, tailored proposal. Contact us to schedule your complimentary session.
